India's performance at the Paris Olympics 2024 has been underwhelming, with only three bronze medals secured so far, placing the nation at the 67th position in the medal standings.The hopes of India's 1.4 billion citizens now rest on the shoulders of Neeraj Chopra, the reigning Olympic champion in javelin throw. After his historic gold medal victory at the Tokyo Olympics, Neeraj has become a national icon, and fans are eagerly anticipating a repeat of his success.
